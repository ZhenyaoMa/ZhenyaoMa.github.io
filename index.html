My Research

Behavior Learning (BL)

Zhenyao Ma, Yue Liang, Dongxu Li · International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR)

Summary

Inspired by behavioral science, we propose Behavior Learning (BL), a novel general-purpose ML framework that learns (hierarchical) optimization structures from data, as a promising alternative to neural network for scientific domains involving optimization.

Contribution

Project leader. Proposed and led the core idea, theoretical framework, and manuscript writing; contributed to coding and experimental implementation.

Human Social Preferences in Simple and Complex Decisions

With Sen Geng and Menglong Guan · Working Paper, 2026

Summary

Social preferences capture human motives beyond self-interest, such as kindness and moral concern. In this, we develop a rational-choice framework that can explain why behavior might appear selfish in some contexts while pro-social in others, even if the underlying social preferences themselves are stable.

Contribution

Co-first and co-corresponding author (alphabetical order). Led the development of the theoretical framework, Significantly advanced and refined the initial idea and experimental design, and Conducted the experimental implementation.
